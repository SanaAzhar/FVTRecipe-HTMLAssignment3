Chicken Biryani

Introduction

I love chicken biryani, and that love pushed me to learn how to make it myself. My first two attempts didn’t go so well. But I kept practicing and learning from each mistake. Now I make a biryani so delicious,both my parents and in-laws love it. And every time, when I visit my parents home they especially request to make Biryani.I find Food Fusion's recipes very delicious, that's why I follow their recipes.

Ingredients

  1. Pyaz (Onion) sliced 1 medium
  2. Tamatar (Tomatoes) sliced 3 medium
  3. Hara dhania (Fresh coriander) chopped ½ Cup
  4. Podina (Mint leaves) chopped ½ Cup
  5. Lemon juice 2 tbs
  6. Cooking oil 1 Cup
  7. Pyaz (Onion) sliced 4 large
  8. Hari elaichi (Green cardamom) 3-4
  9. Badi elaichi (Black cardamom) 2-3
  10. Sabut kali mirch (Black pepper) ½ tsp
  11. Laung (Cloves) 6-7
  12. Darchini (Cinnamon sticks) 2-3
  13. Tez paat (Bay leaves) 2-3
  14. Hari mirch (Green chilies) 3-4
  15. Adrak lehsan paste (Ginger garlic paste) 3 tbs
  16. Dhania powder (Coriander powder) 2 tbs
  17. Haldee powder (Turmeric powder) 1 tsp
  18. Garam masala powder 1 tsp
  19. Lal mirch powder (Red chili powder) 1 & ½ tbs or to taste
  20. Namak (Salt) 2 tsp or to taste
  21. Chicken with bones 1 kg
  22. Chawal (Rice) Sella/Basmati 1 kg (soaked & boiled until ¾ th done, rice partially cooked but not fully softened.)
  23. Javatri powder (Mace powder) ¼ tsp
  24. Jaifil powder (Nutmeg powder) ¼ tsp
  25. Zarda ka rang (Yellow food color) ½ tsp or as required

Cooking Steps

    Boiling Rice:

  • Wash rice well and soak for 20–30 minutes. Boil water with salt, then add rice. Cook until 70–75% done (slightly firm). Drain the water and set aside.

    • Chicken Masala Prepration:

  • In pot,add cooking oil,onion and fry until golden brown (approx. 10-12 minutes) & take out half quantity of fried onion and reserve for later use.
  • Add green cardamom,black cardamom,black pepper,cloves,cinnamon sticks,bay leaves,green chilies and mix well.
  • Add ginger garlic paste and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  • Add water and mix well.
  • Add coriander powder,turmeric powder,garam masala powder,paprika powder,red chili powder,salt and mix well.
  • Add tomato mixture, mix well and cook until tomatoes are soft.
  • Add chicken and mix well until changes color.
  • Cover and cook on low flame for 8-10 minutes then cook on high flame until oil separates and then take half masala into a saperate bowl.
  • Then, add half layer of boiled rice on the Chicken Masala.
  • Add cooked chicken and add remaining boiled rice.
  • In water,add yellow food color and mix well.
  • Ad dissolve food colour and fried onion,cover and steam cook on low flame for 10 minutes.

      • Extra Toppings

    • Open the lid and let the biryani rest for 5 minutes.
    • Carefully transfer the biryani to a serving dish in an even, organized layer.
    • Sprinkle chopped mint leaves on top.
    • Add chopped fresh coriander (dhania).
    • Spread fried onions evenly over the biryani.
    • Decorate with fresh lemon slices for a good presentation and fresh look.
    • Cover for 2–3 minutes before serving to lock in aroma and flavor.
